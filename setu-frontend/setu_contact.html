About Setu

What is Setu?

Setu — meaning “bridge” in Sanskrit — is a real-time collaboration platform that connects people through chat, file sharing, and a shared whiteboard, all inside your browser. No installs, no friction, just connect.

💬

Live Chat

Real-time messaging with replies, edits, unsend, and smart context menus — a modern messenger built right in.

📁

P2P File Sharing

Send files & folders directly to peers — no server storage, no limits. Plus QR-code transfers that auto-delete in 7 minutes.

🎨

Shared Whiteboard

Brainstorm, sketch, and diagram together on a live Excalidraw-powered canvas that syncs to every room member instantly.

🔗

Instant Rooms

Create or join a room in seconds with an 8-character code, a QR code, or a shareable link — auto-reconnect keeps you linked.

🔐

Secure by Design

JWT auth, encrypted transit, and optional password-protected transfers keep your conversations and files private.

📱

Works Everywhere

A responsive, installable PWA with dark & light themes — beautiful on phones, tablets, and desktops alike.
